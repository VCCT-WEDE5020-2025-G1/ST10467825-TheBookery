About Us!

History

Founded in 2017 by Thando Mbeki, a former librarian, The Bookery started as a tiny secondhand bookstore in Observatory, Cape Town. Frustrated by the lack of affordable books and literary spaces in underserved communities, Thando’s vision was to create a culturally inclusive bookstore that celebrates African authors, local storytelling, and multilingual literacy (English, Afrikaans, Zulu). Today, The Bookery hosts township reading programs, partners with schools, and champions South African literature—but struggles with limited reach beyond its physical location.
